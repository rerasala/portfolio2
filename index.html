Lifting Buddy

A beginner lifter's best friend

Lifting buddy is a concept lifting application that I designed, created, and prototyped in Adobe XD. This was my first design projects ever and it remains one that I am extremely proud of. While making the application, I consulted Apple’s Human Interface guidelines to ensure that it not only looked great but was usable too.

Ford E-Travel is an application I designed and prototyped in Figma as a part of the IT Innovation Challenge that took place during my internship at the Ford Motor Company. Wolverine Ticket Transfer

Buy Blue. Go Blue.

Wolverine Ticket Transfer is an application I made for my entrepreneurship 390 class that was designed in Balsamiq and Figma. The goal of this application was to be a one stop hub for all ticket sales for Michigan football games, making the experience of Michigan football that much better.

Design Project: Umbrella

Umbrella is a project I'm currently working on in my Digital Product Design Class. Umbrella is a concept application that will be created in Figma, using Balsamiq for high-fidelity wireframing. Ultiimately, Umbrella will be an application that will help users decide what accesories or outerwear they will need in order to combat incliment weather around them. An intuitive, clean interface, and calendar integration will hopefully make umbrella the weather app for busy people.

Design Project: KTP Rush Web App

KTP is the professional technology fraternity that I am a part of. An important aspect of being a brother in KTP is playing a part in the "rush" process every year that brings in more applicants to KTP. On one night called "Decision Night", brothers must evaluate 40 rushees and select the top candidates to be a part of KTP. In order to view all of the vital information concerning interview scores, application essays, etc., KTP has decided to make a web app that allows brothers to easily view all of this information on decision night. As head of the KTP design commitee, I am currently leading a team of 10 members to design this rush application.
